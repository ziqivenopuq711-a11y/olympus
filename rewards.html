Primacy of Rules
10% of directly affected funds. Applies to unauthorized extraction of treasury assets, unauthorized minting, direct theft of user funds, or kernel bypass. Capped at $3,333,333.
Critical Reward Calculation

For critical vulnerabilities affecting mainnet assets, the reward is calculated as 10% of the funds directly at risk, up to a maximum of $3,333,333.

The calculation uses the average price of OHM between CoinMarketCap.com and CoinGecko.com at the exact time the bug report is submitted. No adjustments are made for market liquidity or slippage.

For example, if a vulnerability puts $5,000,000 of treasury assets at risk, the reward would be $500,000 (10%), paid in OHM at the submission-time price.

Custom Program Tiers (OlympusDAO)

OlympusDAO Three-Tier Reward Structure

This is a Custom Program governed by OlympusDAO. In addition to standard severity levels, the program has three specific tiers:

Tier 1
Loss of Deposited or User Funds
For bugs that would lead to direct loss of deposited user funds. Reward equals the potential loss amount, up to $333,333 USD, paid in OHM.
Tier 2
Direct Economic Loss of Treasury Assets
For bugs leading to direct economic loss of treasury-controlled assets — including unauthorized extraction, improperly discounted purchases, or forced selling below intended pricing. Reward equals the potential loss, up to $3,333,333 USD, paid in OHM.
Tier 3 — Discretionary
High-Quality, High-Impact Submissions
Issued at the discretion of the Bug Bounty Management team for submissions that don't qualify under other tiers but are of exceptional quality and material security value. Up to $16,942. Not available to Olympus contributors. This tier is awarded very rarely — researchers should not assume eligibility.

Payment Process

1
Submit your report
Submit your finding via the Immunefi platform with a working PoC. The team acknowledges receipt within 48 hours.
2
Triage & classification
The Olympus Bug Bounty Management team triages the submission and assigns a severity within 7 business days. You may be asked to provide additional information.
3
Reward agreement
Upon severity agreement, the reward amount is calculated per the program rules. You will receive a reward offer via the Immunefi platform.
4
OHM payment
Payment is processed directly by OlympusDAO in OHM. The OHM amount is calculated using the average of CoinMarketCap and CoinGecko prices at time of submission. No KYC required.
5
Disclosure (optional)
After the fix is deployed and 90 days have elapsed, you may request written approval to publish a public write-up (Category 3 — Approval Required).

Rewards FAQ

How is the OHM reward amount calculated?
The USD reward amount is determined first based on the severity and affected funds. The equivalent OHM amount is then calculated using the average of CoinMarketCap and CoinGecko prices at the moment you submit your bug report.
Can I receive more than the stated maximum?
No. The program operates under Primacy of Rules. The maximum reward for any single submission is $3,333,333 regardless of the potential impact scope. Rewards are not negotiable above the stated caps.
Are Olympus contributors eligible?
No. Active Olympus contributors should report bugs directly to the Bug Bounty Management team via the private Olympus Discord channel and are not eligible for public program rewards.
What if multiple researchers find the same bug?
Only the first valid submission for a specific vulnerability will receive a reward. Duplicate reports are not rewarded. Researchers are encouraged to submit as soon as a bug is confirmed reproducible.
Is there a minimum reward amount?
There is no defined minimum. Low-severity findings that meet the in-scope criteria will be evaluated for a reward. Findings that don't meet the impact threshold will be acknowledged but may not receive a monetary reward.
