Key Protocol Concepts

What is the Olympus Default Framework?
Default is Olympus's modular smart contract framework. It separates protocol logic into a Kernel, Modules (storing state), and Policies (implementing logic). Researchers should understand how roles and permissions flow between these components when assessing vulnerabilities.
How does OHM bonding work?
Bonding allows users to sell assets (e.g., ETH, DAI, LP tokens) to the Olympus Treasury at a discount in exchange for OHM vested over a fixed term. The Bond Depository and Bond Aggregator manage these transactions. Key attack vectors include price manipulation, bond mispricing, and early redemption exploits.
What is Range-Bound Stability (RBS)?
RBS is Olympus's on-chain stability mechanism that uses treasury reserves to maintain OHM price within a defined range. It involves Cushion and Wall price levels managed by the PRICE and RANGE modules. Vulnerabilities in oracle manipulation or capacity accounting are particularly relevant here.
How are protocol-owned liquidity positions secured?
Olympus manages Uniswap V3 and Balancer LP positions through its LIQUIDITY module and BunniHub integration. Unauthorized withdrawal of liquidity, fee theft, or manipulation of liquidity positions are in-scope critical-severity impacts.

Responsible Disclosure Policy

Olympus adheres to Responsible Publication Category 3 — Approval Required. This means researchers must obtain explicit written approval from the OlympusDAO Bug Bounty Management team before publishing, presenting, or otherwise disclosing any details of a submitted vulnerability.

After a bug is submitted and triaged:

  • The team will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
  • A severity classification will be provided within 7 days
  • Upon agreement on severity and reward, payment is processed in OHM
  • Researchers may request approval to publish a write-up 90 days after the fix is deployed on mainnet

Unauthorized disclosure prior to approval will result in disqualification from the current reward and may affect future program eligibility. The Immunefi platform facilitates all communications and dispute resolution under the terms of OIP-38.
